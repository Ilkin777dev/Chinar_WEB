MƏKAN

TERRAS

Provides a vibrant and refreshing setting, featuring an outdoor terrace surrounded by a bamboo.

BAMBOO BAR

The Bamboo Bar is the perfect setting for a chilled beer or a specialty cocktail. Decorated with bamboo plants and life-sized terracotta warriors imported from the Xian region of China, it offers a unique and immersive atmosphere.

PAN ASIAN OTAĞI

This place features a contemporary design blending local styles with Asian influences. It boasts a spacious open kitchen with dedicated areas. The décor, highlighted by elegant bamboo and robust tree, creates a unique and inviting dining atmosphere.

DRAGON LOUNGE

Taking inspiration from dragons, mulberry, silk, and fire, the design reflects cultural elements shared by Azerbaijan and China; and reimagines them in a superb contemporary environment of refined detail and luxurious materials.

DINING ROOMS

Our two luxurious dining rooms offer guests a unique design featuring wooden dragons and a fireplace covered in 24-carat gold. Here, you can enjoy complete privacy and top-level personalized service. With the option of combining the rooms into a large space, they have been designed with unique interactive ceiling recreating the dappling effect of the sun through tree foliage. Parties can define different moods according to the event – from a formal business lunch to a relaxed social evening or family occasion.

WINE CELLAR

The fully glazed walk-in wine and sake cellar stores a large variety of premium labels ready for serving.The comprehensive selection is sourced from the best vineyards in the world and houses the most extensive range of sake in Baku.
