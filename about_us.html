Tradition and Innovation

CHiNAR is more than just a restaurant; it is a living embodiment of history and culinary art, reimagined for the modern world. Each dish here is a harmony of traditional recipes and contemporary culinary techniques, creating an unparalleled gastronomic journey.



Immerse yourself in an atmosphere of refined taste and cultural richness, and enjoy the finest dishes prepared with love and expertise.

THE PHILOSOPHY OF CHiNAR

We believe in harmony – in our flavors, with nature, and in our dining experience. Each meal at CHiNAR is more than just food. it's a celebration crafted with the finest local and exotic ingredients, honoring the true spirit of Asian cuisine.

Meet Our Culinary Team

At CHiNAR, every dish has a story of tradition and creativity. Our team, led by a top chef and passionate staff, is the core of our restaurant. They craft authentic Asian flavors with a modern touch.

THE VENUE

TERRACE

The terrace of the CHiNAR Restaurant offers a unique dining experience. Surrounded by lush greenery and elegant bamboo accents, it provides a serene and sophisticated ambiance.

BAMBOO BAR

The Bamboo Bar is the perfect setting for a cold beer or a specialty cocktail. Decorated with bamboo plants and life-sized terracotta warriors imported from the Xian region of China, it offers a unique and immersive atmosphere.

PAN ASIAN DINING

This place features a contemporary design blending local styles with Asian influences. It boasts a spacious open kitchen with dedicated areas. The décor, highlighted by elegant bamboo and robust tree, creates a unique and inviting dining atmosphere.

DRAGON LOUNGE

Taking inspiration from dragons, mulberry, silk, and fire, the design reflects cultural elements shared by Azerbaijan and China; and reimagines them in a superb contemporary environment of refined detail and luxurious materials.

DINING ROOMS

Our two luxurious dining rooms offer guests a unique design featuring wooden dragons and a fireplace covered in 24-carat gold. Here, you can enjoy complete privacy and top-level personalized service. With the option of combining the rooms into a large space, they have been designed with unique interactive ceiling recreating the dappling effect of the sun through tree foliage. Parties can define different moods according to the event – from a formal business lunch to a relaxed social evening or family occasion.

WINE CELLAR

The fully glazed walk-in wine and sake cellar stores a large variety of premium labels ready for serving.The comprehensive selection is sourced from the best vineyards in the world and houses the most extensive range of sake in Baku.
