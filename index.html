CHiNAR has been famous Baku landmark and meeting place for decades. Today CHiNAR offers a range of dining and entertainment experiences that are amongst the finest in the world.

CHiNAR is inspired by the mythical azeri hero Bahram Gur who slaved a formidable dragon. Also, near CHiNAR is the monument dedicated to this hero.

Originally tea house under a cluster of CHiNAR trees, CHiNAR has now been reborn as a magnificent building of contemporary architecture.
